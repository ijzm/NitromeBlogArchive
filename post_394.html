Youtube Videos UpdateTue, 12 Apr 2011

We are as always reviewing elements of our blog and as we have been adding a lot of Youtube clips recently, we are seeing some undesirable results. A few people have reported problems seeing the videos and there is a known issue of the video teariJng at the edges of the viewport which currently seems unfixable.

There is also the problem that you can’t drag through the videos which is a problem with other items also and an issue we hope to address in the future. We have decided, due to these issues that we should change the way we implement videos into our blog and therefore all future videos will be accessed via a link to the video page to keep the process cleaner!
