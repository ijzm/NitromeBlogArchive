Play Abduction!Mon, 11 Feb 2013

We've chosen Abduction by Fliptic as todays pixel love game!

This game has some really nice artwork and animations with some really fun gameplay. Your task is to amuse your alien abductors by completing a series of puzzles.

We enjoyed playing through and defeating the number of obstacles that quickly ramp up to thwart your success.

Be advised that there is a cartoonish 'head pop' animation that, although animated wonderfully by the artist, may not be for some of our fans.
