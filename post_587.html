Rainbogeddon Released!Thu, 02 Feb 2012

Hi Nitromians

You may remember this game was featured as a preview a few months back and finally we now have our game Rainbogeddon ready for you all to play!

This is Nitromes take on the maze munching genre established by Pacman and more recently reimagined by the likes of iOS game Forget-me-not and indeed Namco’s own Pacman C.E .

While we have kept the main aim of munching pixels to finish each level we have focused on adding variety to the format with different monster (ghost?) types, some new level elements and most notably by the inclusion of several power ups which vastly alter the gameplay!

Drill through the level with the drill attachment, teleport from enemies with a handy placement of your teleporter, create protective bubble zones to hide in and more.
