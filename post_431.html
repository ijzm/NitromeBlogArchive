Some Test Subject News!Tue, 24 May 2011

Good day to you Nitromians!
We’re pleased to hear that many of you liked Test Subject Green. A lot of you have been asking when the 3rd instalment of the Test Subject series will be coming out. So we wanted to take this opportunity to let you know that it will be done, but the gap will be longer than the one between Test Subject Blue and Test Subject Green.

JK and Markus are developing new games first and then will work on Test Subject …”3”. We do however have a little surprise for you all. We have a smaller Test Subject spin off game coming out in the not too distant future that answers some of your requests! We’ve had a lot of fun making and testing it and we hope you guys will enjoy it too! So watch this space!
Keep having fun gaming!
