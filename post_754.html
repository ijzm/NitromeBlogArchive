Play One Button Bob!Mon, 19 Nov 2012

Now we know there are a few games like this out there but One Button Bob deserves recognition due to it’s slick execution.

Easy to pick up and play, each challenge is different enough to keep you interested. With the difficulty level set to “fun” rather than “rock hard and frustrating!” we wanted to shine our weekly light of love on Tom “Ninjadoodle” Vencel’s pixelated adventure.

Oh and we love the Commodore 64 reference at the start… extra kudos for that!
It looks good, sounds good and plays well. So go on give it ten minutes of your time and enjoy :0)
