Play Perspective!Mon, 28 Jan 2013

This week's pixel love comes in the form a very endearing game in which you play as the pet of an elderly lady.

Perspective by nfyre.

The game asks you to look at the enemies and blocks in the environment with a change of perspective.

As you progress through the levels you find out more and more about the characters and the friendship which they share.

The game has well made, laid back ambient music, which really adds to the games atmosphere and depth.

Perspective: Click to Play!

We hope you all enjoy this as much as we did!

by Tom
