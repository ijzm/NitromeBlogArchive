Play Llama in your Face!Mon, 22 Oct 2012

It’s another gloomy looking Monday over here in London UK, so we thought we would try and brighten things up a bit with today’s Pixelove ‘Llama in your face’ a zany defence game from Big Dino developed by Simon Parzer and Peter Sperl.

This game is every bit as crazy as it’s title sound’s; you take control of a Llama who must drive away the hordes of visitors before they reach his cage. Your only defence is to spit at them and send them away in a slime filled rage.

It’s a game that is built on a simple premise of point, aim and click but it builds upon this over its 18 rounds to become something which is both engrossing and bags of fun.

Quickly collect money with your mouse and upgrade your defences such as multi-target, spit guns, battle cry, power-up helicopters and more. Or try out the games separate Arena mode and aim to beat your personal best.

There is plenty to love here, from the tiny and charming sprites to the great environments and sound which makes a very highly polished experience and one that we highly recommend to brighten up your day.
