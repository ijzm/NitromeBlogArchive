Play Sydney Hunter!Mon, 29 Jul 2013

Hi Nitromians!

This week's pixel love game is Sydney Hunter created by Keith Erickson of Studio Pina!

We really enjoyed this adventure game. The view distance was handled cleverly with the viewable area fading into a deep blue tone. It adds a real sense of urgency when trying to aim your boomerang attacks.

The music and sound effects fit in very well with this game and accent the retro graphics elegantly. There is a lot of fun to be had boomeranging the enemies and collecting the items in this one!
