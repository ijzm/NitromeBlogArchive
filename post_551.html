Nitrome Must Die UpdateFri, 25 Nov 2011

After reading all your comments regarding Nitrome Must Die, we thought we'd elaborate on a few things...

First of all we'd like thank all those who gave us lots of constructive criticism and support for the game. We wanted to point out a couple of things that may have confused a few of you.

Level saving:- Levels are grouped into sets of 10, your levels are saved after each boss is beaten. This is because we wanted a certain amount of randomisation to the game to add to the replay value and also to keep the score gamble element unpredictable.

Levels unlocking:- There aren't any levels specifically that can be unlocked once completed, within each set there are more levels than 10 therefore each time you play you might experience a different game. We've been hard at work fixing some of the bugs and will continue to do so after the weekend if anymore occur, so please bear with us.

Nitrome Must Die fixes include:
- In game help screen
- Level Select Screen (with a little more explanation)
- Player keys are saved for when players come back to the game
- Play key selection has been made a little easier to use. So if player 1 wants to use the arrow keys it won't stop them.
- Volume changes to some sound effects and a couple of other in-game bugs that were mentioned

Hope you enjoy Nitrome Must Die, have a great weekend!
