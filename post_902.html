Bug Fixing - Accounts Update!Thu, 25 Jul 2013

Hi Everyone!

We are steadily moving through the list of bugs that we have found within the accounts system. Please feel free to add comments reporting more bugs below or over on Facebook.

The more we know about your experiences, the better we can diagnose problems. Try to give us your operating system and browser version when reporting bugs so that we can better pin point errors.

Issues that we have resolved:

  • You can now unlock the avatar with the hint - "You could do with being a little more... social? Why not invite some friends round!"
  • You can collect the "Heart 100 Games" badge
  • The icon games will now work within their icons on the home page and game selection pages.
  • In the blog/all comments sections, you can now click on the top level user avatar to be taken to their profile page.
  • We have added the login box to more pages on the site. There are still some missing. We will get round to all pages.
  • We have fixed various redirection bugs in the profile section of the site.
  • We have cleaned up some style issues on the avatars and badge pages.

We will be retrofitting the badge and avatar mentioned above to users possibly tomorrow or next week. So if you feel you have performed the correct actions to gain these, you could be unlocking a new avatar/badge soon! We'll let you know as soons as we roll out this update :)

Thanks for helping us get the bugs fixed. We will keep you informed on any more updates!
