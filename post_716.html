Play Corporate Climber!Mon, 01 Oct 2012

This week’s Pixel love goes out to the highly talented crew over at PixelJam. Corporate Climber a highly enjoyable platformer created for Adultswim.

The aim of the game is to climb the company’s office floor by floor, slowly working your way up through the ranks until you reach the top of the corporate ladder.

This has been a huge office favourite for some time now, as there is just so much to like about it. From the clever pixelated office environments to the humorous and dark depiction of a huge corporate company. If you haven’t tried this game then it’s well worth the 20 mins or so it will take you to reach the end.

...PLEASE NOTE...
For those of you who are faint of heart there are several instances of pixelated deaths (death by poop is no joke). And some objectionable content throughout. Don’t say we didn’t warn you!
