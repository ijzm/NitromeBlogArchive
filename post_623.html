Cheese Dreams Demo Update!Fri, 13 Apr 2012

Hi Nitromians!

The core control mechanic has been updated in the Cheese Dreams Demo due to some of the helpful comments we received from our fans via the Facebook page.

We have changed the way the player reacts to bouncing on slopes. This change means it should be much easier to control yourself on pivoting platforms. We have updated the demo, so why not try out the improved control mechanic and let us know your thoughts?

Thanks for helping us during the games development! If you would like to give us further feedback, feel free to join in using Facebook, Twitter or Email!
