Icebreaker GamesMaster ReviewWed, 24 Jul 2013

Those lovely people at GamesMaster Magazine have included a glowing review of Icebreaker: A Viking Voyage in their latest edition that hit the UK newsagent shelves a couple of days ago.

GamesMaster magazine is a well respected multi-format videogame magazine that has been running for over 20 years now. We all have fond memories of first buying the magazine in our younger years and continue to do so now. So we are really chuffed to see Icebreaker receive such a favourable review.

So if you fancy it, (and you live in the UK) pop down to your local newsagent and pick up a copy.
