Hi Nitromians,

Today's Pixel Love game is Give Up, Robot which was created by Matt Thorson.

The antagonist in this one will be resoundingly similar to the Genetic Lifeform and Disk Operating System (a.k.a GladOS) from Portal.

You navigate the levels with your awesome grappling rope skills that you may have picked up from our game, Final Ninja.

We really like the haziness and psychedelic style of the artwork in this game. The sound design is also done very well with the antagonist sounding very cold with use of vocoded audio.

A quick word of warning:
The game makes use of vibrant flashing colours and lights in it's level design.

We hope you enjoy this as much as we do!
