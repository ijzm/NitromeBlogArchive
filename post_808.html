Play Qoosh!Mon, 04 Feb 2013

Time for some Pixel Love!

This week we have decided to highlight the awesome game Qoosh developed by Aethos Games!

Veterans of the Portal series will be in somewhat familiar territory here in that you are tasked with solving a series of tests.

The game progresses very elegantly with the puzzle complexity intuitively rising so that you'll find yourself having many 'Aha!' moments throughout.

We really love the dialogue with the antagonist in this too!
