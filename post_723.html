Flightless Demo Released!Mon, 15 Oct 2012

Ok so hopefully we managed to surprise you with this!

Flightless will be, with your help, our first full PC download game available through Steam!
The game is an epic puzzle metrovania platform game for one or two players drop in drop out co-op split screen! You play as ducks that have had their wings clipped for stealing and to pay it back you must search for gems to pay back your debt in a huge dungeon full of monsters and traps.

With clipped wings flying and even jumping are not possible but luckily you have a magic ladder to help you out. Press the action key and it will cast a ladder upwards or downwards until it hits something.

Use this to climb round the levels, take out enemies, trigger switches, weigh down pressure pads, bend round corners (what?) and much more!

Did we mention you can play this game full screen and that there are no ads…..what!!! No really as this is intended as a free flash game we are going all out to promote it!
