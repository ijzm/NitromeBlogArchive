Today's pixel love game comes in the form of a tactical shooter, Counter Terror by Moon Loop!

This is quite a fun little shooter with plenty of objects and scenery pieces to smash through.

The dialogue is also really fun to read through thanks to the comedy value that it holds. The graphics are well drawn too

A quick word of warning though, this game contains some red pixels that represent blood :)
