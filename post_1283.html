Cooped Up Preview 2Thu, 12 Mar 2015

Back at the end of 2014 we had an idea for a small game that we were calling 'Feeding Time', so we started to develop the engine and draw the characters... now we're mid-March it's all changed many, many, MANY times!

Now the game is nearing completion we thought we'd share with you a little nibble of what you can expect.

The game will now be called 'Cooped Up'

We think the development journey whilst has been far longer than we expected, is actually quite interesting and we will share with you different sides to the design adventure as we near the release.

So here's the pitch...

You are the new bird in a caged environment, all the other inhabitants have gone a bit loopy, they've just been cooped up too long! You must survive by twanging between the perches and maybe snack on a few bugs along the way!
