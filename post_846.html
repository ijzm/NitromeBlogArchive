Play Minibot!Mon, 15 Apr 2013

Time for a Pixel Love Game!

Do you remember when we featured the puzzle game Abduction? Well, the creators of that have made yet another awesome game called Minibot!

You play through as the mini robot tasked to cure the virus inside a young girl.

You create the extra platform blocks, which are needed to complete the levels, by absorbing and normalizing different materials. This means each material gives your blocks different properties (such as turning the platform block into a spring).

The graphics are very well drawn, animated and the puzzles are well thought out. We had a lot of fun playing this one.
