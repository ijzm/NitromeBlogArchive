More Bug Fixes!Tue, 06 Aug 2013

Hey everyone,

We have fixed some more account bugs! Just to keep you all up to date with what we feel has been fixed, check out this list.

Account Bug Fixes:

  • Private Messages should now display correctly. Some people have reported to us that their messages were disappearing for no reason. This should now be resolved! 
  • Unhearting games should also work as expected. If you have tried to unheart a game and it didn't remove itself from your list, try hearting and unhearting it again and it should work.
  • The "Touchy" skin has also been fixed. We received reports that the skin was not working as intended and was only showing a small tile when viewed on the "view skin" page. This should now be working properly.

We still have more bugs to fix, thanks to everyone for reporting them to us!

As always, if you have any more issues with accounts that you'd like to share with us, feel free to send a message, write a comment or tell us on our Facebook Page!
