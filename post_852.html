We are looking for C++ developers!Wed, 24 Apr 2013

We are looking for new C++ developers to join our team at Nitrome making games for Mobile and PC/Mac. As a lot of the games are mobile a knowledge of Objective-C is also beneficial and candidates used to using Cocos2d & Box2d would be ideal though this is not essential. Knowledge of source control (especially git) would also be a plus.

For a good idea of the sort of games you would be working on please check out the following two titles that are in production: 

Icebreaker - A Viking Voyage (iPhone/iPod Touch/iPad/Android)

Flightless (Steam/PC/Mac)

All candidates must be able to show examples of their work to be considered. Salary is negotiable. The position requires you work at the Nitrome office in London so candidates must live within commuting distance or be willing to relocate for the role. Please send CV's and Example links to jobs@nitrome.com with the subject line 'programmer application'.
