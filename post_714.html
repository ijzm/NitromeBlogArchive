Hi Nitromians!

It's been a while since we last posted some of your great fanart on our blog and we just have to say a massive thanks to everyone that sends in new art to us, as we absolutely love the pieces that we get sent in.

Today we thought we'd show you something awesome by a Nitromian who goes by the name of Nalthar! It's a great big collage of Nitrome character faces!

We had a lot of fun looking through and seeing all of our characters in this fresh vector format. We also think this could make a pretty cool wallpaper for your computer screens!

Thanks Nalthar, great work! :D

If you have any fan art you have made about Nitrome please do send it in! We love everything we get sent especially if it is our work applied to something we have never seen before. We will feature the best, most striking and most unusual pieces in our blog.
