The Bats Are Back!Mon, 25 Jul 2011

Hi Nitromians!

We recently went back inside the code for one of our older games, Cave Chaos, to fix a few little things when we came across something rather unusual.

It seems that the hard working bats in the game had decided to take a break from delivering the platform pieces and obstacles and take a nap. But thanks to the persuasive efforts of our new games modder Peter, the bats are now back and well rested!

No animals were harmed in the fixing of this game!
