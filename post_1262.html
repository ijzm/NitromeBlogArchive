Two Pixel Love Games!Mon, 02 Feb 2015

Hi Nitromians,

This week, as we didn't highlight any games for Pixel Love last week, we have found two for you to play!

Puzzle Legends

We chose Puzzle Legends as the pixel art looks great and there are some great puzzle elements for you to master. The music is also wonderful!

Boomerang Chang

Boomerang Chang is a very difficult survival game in which you can only throw your boomerangs to the right. In order to stop enemies from the left, you need to limber up and jump over your boomerang! This is quite a fun mechanic that we had good fun playing around with.
