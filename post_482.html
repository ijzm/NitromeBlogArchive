Chrome issues in Windows 7Fri, 12 Aug 2011

Hi Nitromians!

We have recently received some emails regarding problems with controlling older keyboard based games. We have looked into the issue and have found it to be only related to our older (AS2) games.

The problem is linked to the Chrome browser on Windows 7. Google are currently looking into the problem and will be rolling out the update in a week or two updating many of you automatically!

In the meantime, you can play our games on many other popular browsers including Firefox,  Internet Explorer and Safari.
